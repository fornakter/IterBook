IterBook is a speed reading application using the RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) technique.

Features:
- Import books from PDF and TXT files
- Adjustable reading speed (words per minute)
- Pause at sentence endings for better comprehension
- Save reading progress automatically
- Resume reading from where you left off
- Screen stays awake during reading
- Multi-language support (English, Polish)
- Dark and light theme support

RSVP displays words one at a time in a fixed position, eliminating eye movement and allowing for faster reading speeds while maintaining comprehension.

This app is free, open-source, and contains no ads or tracking.
